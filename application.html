Filling Out the Loan Application Documents

  • 1
    Download and print the form on clean A4 sized papers and ensure the print is clear and legible with no missing parts or cut-offs.
  • 2
    Clearly fill out the form using a blue pen. Your signature must be clear and consistent with any official identification documents you may provide.
  • 3
    Ensure your employer fills in their section of the form clearly and signs it where required. The form cannot be processed without your employer's signature.

Submission Process and Account Registration

Once you have completed your loan application, you can submit it through our online submission portal by uploading a scanned copy of your form and supporting documents. After submission, you will be automatically registered on our website, granting you access to a private dashboard where you can monitor your loan repayment status, view payment history, and update your contact information.
